Travel Blog Site Plan

Site Name

Venture Vista Travel Blog - This name was selected because the site will feature content related to travel experiences, tips, and guides for adventurers.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide readers with information about travel destinations, advice on how to plan trips, and personal experiences from different locations. Visitors will find traveling information, tips for saving money while traveling, and recommendations for unique places to explore.

Scenarios

  • How can I plan a budget-friendly vacation?
  • How do I find affordable flights?
  • What are the best travel apps to use?
  • How can I stay safe while traveling alone?
  • What should I know before visiting a new country?
